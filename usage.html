WeatherVue Usage Guide

Welcome to our weather app !! Below are the instructions for searching weather conditions

  1. Enter the name of your city into the search bar
  2. Click on the "SEARCH" button or press "ENTER" on the keyboard
  3. Wait a moment for app to retreive the weather data for your location
  4. You will get the desired results
